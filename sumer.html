Biography

Where regulation, business, and strategy meet.

Sumer's practice sits at the intersection of tax, business, regulatory, and administrative law. She counsels entrepreneurs, closely held businesses, and regulated operators on strategic business and compliance matters throughout every stage of growth.

Her work includes entity formation, corporate planning, regulatory compliance, risk mitigation, operational strategy, and helping businesses navigate the unique challenges of highly regulated industries.

Known for her analytical rigor and practical guidance, Sumer focuses on helping clients understand both the legal framework governing their operations and the broader business implications of strategic decisions.

She prioritizes clarity, preparation, and long-term planning, ensuring clients are positioned to respond effectively to regulatory change and evolving market conditions.

Education & Background

A foundation built on legal and international study.

Sumer earned her Juris Doctor from the University of Florida Levin College of Law, where she developed a strong foundation in corporate, tax, and administrative law.

She also studied international law at Renmin University of China and The Hague Academy of International Law, broadening her understanding of international legal systems, governance structures, and regulatory frameworks.

This combination of domestic and international legal education provides valuable perspective when advising businesses operating in rapidly changing and heavily regulated environments.
