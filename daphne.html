Biography

Protecting innovation. Building brands.

Daphne Benford-Smith, Esq., serves as Senior Counsel at Signature Law Partners, PLLC, where she leads complex intellectual property portfolios and advises clients on strategic legal issues involving trademarks, copyrights, technology, artificial intelligence, commercial transactions, and business operations.

Her practice focuses heavily on clients operating within the video game, animation, entertainment, and broader creative industries. She guides companies, creators, and entrepreneurs through trademark and copyright registration, prosecution, portfolio management, enforcement, litigation, and long-term brand strategy.

In addition to intellectual property matters, Daphne advises clients on high-value commercial transactions, labor and employment issues, and broader business concerns from a general counsel perspective.

Her practical, business-oriented approach helps clients protect valuable assets while positioning themselves for sustainable growth and innovation.

Experience & Education

Built on legal excellence and practical experience.

Prior to joining Signature Law Partners, Daphne served as Managing Attorney at Benford-Smith Law Firm, where she led trademark prosecution efforts, advised on brand portfolio strategy, and handled sophisticated commercial transactions for clients across multiple industries.

Earlier in her career, she gained significant experience in family law and served as a law clerk within the Harris County Civil Courts, providing legal analysis across a broad range of complex litigation matters.

Daphne earned her Juris Doctor from Texas Southern University's Thurgood Marshall School of Law, where she served as Executive Editor of the Law Journal on Gender, Race and Justice and was recognized multiple times for academic excellence through Dean's List honors.

She received her Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from the University of North Texas, where she was recognized as a top moot court speaker and held numerous leadership positions.

Community & Creative Leadership

Deeply connected to the communities she serves.

Daphne serves as an Advisory Board Member of Women In Games International and is an active member of Texas Bar College. She is passionate about supporting innovation, inclusion, and entrepreneurship within gaming, technology, and creative industries.

Outside her legal practice, Daphne is a content creator, Pokémon streamer, and enthusiastic supporter of gaming, anime, music, travel, and logic puzzles.

She is also a proud and active member of Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc., where she regularly leads community initiatives, educational programming, and service projects.
