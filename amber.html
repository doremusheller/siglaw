Amber M. Naiman

Innovative corporate transactional attorney helping businesses, entrepreneurs, investors, and operators navigate complex transactions, regulatory environments, and strategic growth opportunities.

Biography

Turning complexity into opportunity.

Amber M. Naiman advises established, emerging, and startup companies throughout every stage of the business life cycle. Her practice focuses on a broad range of corporate and commercial transactions, helping clients build, grow, restructure, acquire, and position businesses for long-term success.

Her clients operate across diverse industries including Food & Beverage, Retail & Hospitality, Agriculture, E-Commerce, Regulated Industries, and Emerging Growth Markets.

Amber is widely recognized for her practical, comprehensive, and business-first approach. She works closely with clients to transform complex legal and operational challenges into clear, actionable business solutions.

Throughout much of her legal career, Amber has been deeply involved in highly regulated and emerging industries, providing strategic counsel to operators, retailers, manufacturers, investors, landlords, startups, multi-state businesses, and ancillary service providers.

Regulated Industries

Experienced guidance in evolving legal environments.

Amber has extensive experience advising clients operating within highly regulated and emerging industries, helping organizations understand regulatory obligations, manage risk, and capitalize on new opportunities.

Her work includes industry-specific compliance analysis, multi-state regulatory strategy, jurisdictional surveys, legislative monitoring, and practical implementation planning.

Amber routinely assists clients in addressing novel legal issues, evaluating risk, answering complex regulatory questions, and developing creative solutions tailored to unique business circumstances.

By combining industry knowledge with business acumen, she helps clients successfully navigate fast-paced, demanding, and constantly evolving legal landscapes.

Education & Credentials

Built on legal and business foundations.

Amber earned her Juris Doctor from the University of Denver Sturm College of Law and later completed a Master of Laws (LL.M.) in International Business Transactions from the same institution.

She also earned a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Comm.) from the University of Guelph in Ontario, Canada, providing a strong business foundation that continues to inform her legal practice today.

Amber has been licensed to practice law in Colorado since 2016 and earned Mediation Training and Mentoring Certification in 2018.
