Biography

Business counsel built on experience.

Matthew A. Rivela is a Founding Partner at Signature Law Partners, PLLC. For more than twenty years, he has represented businesses, entrepreneurs, investors, executives, and high-profile individuals in complex transactional and litigation matters.

His practice focuses on mergers and acquisitions, commercial litigation, corporate law, and strategic business counseling. Throughout his career, Matthew has advised clients across a broad range of industries on acquisitions, divestitures, governance matters, financing transactions, contract negotiations, and business growth initiatives.

He has structured, negotiated, and closed transactions with an aggregate value exceeding $3 billion, representing buyers, sellers, investors, and companies in sophisticated strategic transactions.

Matthew's practical, business-oriented approach enables clients to identify opportunities, mitigate risk, and maximize value while remaining focused on long-term objectives.
